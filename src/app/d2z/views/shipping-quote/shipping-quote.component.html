Sea Freight

Taking on the world by importing or exporting means coming face to face with complex international regulations. Our team provide a total service covering the sea freight movement of your product, customs clearance and tariff classification, coordinating pick-up and delivery, all operated by our professional team of international sea freight specialists.

D2Z Logistics offer worldwide Import and Export Services to clients from every industry and for all types of cargo. We provide a dedicated, personalised service with competitive shipping rates, giving you peace of mind that your goods will arrive on time and within budget.

Our Sea Freight Services Include:

  • LCL Consolidation Services.
  • FCL Services via Direct and Transhipment Carriers.
  • Door to Door Services.
